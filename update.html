Unzip the staff zip, then reload the extension.

  1. Find nosey-little-bird-*-staff.zip in your Downloads.
  2. Unzip it. Replace the files in your current bird folder
    (the one you Load unpacked — often nosey-little-bird-v2.0.0).
  3. Open brave://extensions (or chrome://extensions).
  4. Click Reload on Nosey Little Bird.

Brave may block opening brave:// from this page — paste it in the address bar if the link does nothing.
